Who?, What?, When?, Why?, Where?🤔

Don’t worry we will answer all the 5 W’s you have about us 😜. Down Below

What?

Kapsabet Film Club is one of the many clubs in Kapsabet Boys High School and not to brag probably the best club in Kapsabet High School 🥳. Kapsabet has, is and will produce more outstanding and awesome 😎 films “of all time”. You can watch some of our films on our YouTube channel📹. Simply call our club the G.O.A.T 🐐 of Kenyan high school film making. #tomorrowsFilmMakerz

Where?

Kapsabet Film Club is located in Kapsabet Boys High School which is situated in Nandi Hills ⛰, Kenya

When?

Kapsabet Film Club was officially conceived after the full adequate training program facilitated by the Kenya Film Commission on xx/xx/2022

Why?

Kapsabet Film Club was established to nurture the young and overflowing talent in the film industry in Kenya so as to prepare #tomorrowsFilmMakerz

Who?

Kapsabet Film Club has a total of almost 100 members 🙀 who are highly dedicated to the club success. Members of the KB film Club were adequately trained by the Kenya Film Commission on various aspects in the film making process like editing, cinematography, scriptwriting and many other skills The KB Film Club is very proud of it’s very awesome members who have stayed with the club through its ups😃 and downs😭
